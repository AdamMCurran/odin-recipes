My Fried Rice

How to: Make Fried Rice my way!

I love fried rice mixtures of all varieties! This is something I've been eating lately!

Ingredients (Serves 4):

  • 1 Quart Of Day Old White Rice
  • 1 Bag Of Mixed Vegetables
  • 6 Baby Bella Mushrooms, Thinly Sliced
  • 1 Half Large Yellow Onion, Diced
  • 4 Slices Of Thick Pork Roll, Diced
  • 3 Cloves Of Garlic, Minced
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 2 Ounces of Soy Sauce
  • Salt And Pepper

Step-by-Step:

Rice:

  1. Thoroughly wash rice in a strainer.
  2. Add rice to a small-medium pot, add water until just an inch over rice.
  3. Set burner to medium heat, cover rice with a lid, cook for 15 minutes.
  4. After 15 minutes, remove from heat, stir rice, re-lid and place to the side for 5 minutes.
  5. Add rice to a container, place in fridge over night.

Next day:

  1. Add oil to a large non-stick skillet, medium-high heat.
  2. Add pork roll, cook until slightly brown.
  3. Add onion and mushrooms, slightly brown.
  4. Salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Add garlic, slightly brown.
  6. Add mixed veggies, mix around skillet.
  7. Add oil as necessary.
  8. Add day old rice, mix around skillet.
  9. Add soy sauce, mix.
  10. Crack two eggs into rice, mix thoroughly as they cook.
  11. Serve in bowls!
